About MasterTech Auto Repair Center

At MasterTech Auto Repair Center, we take pride in being a family-owned business that has been serving the San Antonio area for over two decades. Our mission is simple: to provide honest, reliable, and high-quality auto repair services that our customers can trust. Whether it’s routine maintenance, complex repairs, or diagnostics, our certified technicians are equipped with the latest tools and technology to ensure your vehicle runs smoothly and safely.

We understand that your car is more than just a means of transportation—it’s a part of your daily life. That’s why we treat every vehicle as our own, with the utmost care and attention to detail. From brake services to engine repairs, we stand behind our work with integrity and transparency, making sure you receive the best possible service every time.

Thank you for choosing MasterTech Auto Repair Center. We are committed to earning your trust, one repair at a time.

Services We Offer

Brake Repair

Ensure your safety with our comprehensive brake repair services. Our skilled technicians will inspect, diagnose, and repair your brake system to ensure it functions correctly, providing you with peace of mind on the road.

Oil Changes

Keep your engine running smoothly with regular oil changes. Our oil change service includes a thorough inspection of your vehicle's engine, ensuring it remains in optimal condition and prolonging its lifespan.

Tire Rotation

Extend the life of your tires with our tire rotation services. Regular tire rotation helps to ensure even tire wear, improving your vehicle's handling and safety while maximizing the lifespan of your tires.

Our Process

Step 1: Schedule

Book your appointment online or give us a call. Our user-friendly online booking system allows you to schedule your service at your convenience, and our friendly staff is always ready to assist you over the phone.

Step 2: Drop Off

Drop off your vehicle at our service center. Our team will greet you and ensure that all your service needs are understood and documented. We strive to make the drop-off process as quick and seamless as possible.

Step 3: Pick Up

Pick up your vehicle once the service is complete. We will notify you when your vehicle is ready, and our team will provide a detailed explanation of the services performed. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
